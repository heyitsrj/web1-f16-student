My Goals


For my portfolio website I want a simple homepage. There will be a navigation bar that will have links leading to a contact page, about page, and portfolio page. I want to show my strongest work in my portfolio, and I want the page to look professional and fun. I want it to represent my graphic design work and who I am as a person. It will have a simple design with some color so that there aren't too many distraction elements on the page.
