About The Event

This is Boston's biggest rock music festival where many artists come to perform. Bands from different genres such as Alternative, punk, and soft rock.

The festival lasts for three days, it starts Friday, May 18 at 10:30 am, and ends Sunday, May 21 at 11:30 pm.
